TypeTalk AI

Advanced Voice Typing & AI Assistant for NVDA

TypeTalk AI is an intelligent voice typing assistant built specifically for NVDA users. It is powered by Google Speech Services and advanced Artificial Intelligence models to ensure high-accuracy dictation and smart text processing.

Features

  • Smart Dictation: High-accuracy speech-to-text using Google Speech Recognition.
  • AI Intelligence: Automatic grammar correction and punctuation.
  • Translation: Real-time translation to English and other languages.
  • Emoji Support: Context-aware AI-suggested emojis.
  • Offline Mode: Basic spoken punctuation support.

How to Use

  1. Press NVDA + Shift + Space to activate command mode.
  2. Press S to Start/Stop dictation.
  3. Press H to open the Help menu.
